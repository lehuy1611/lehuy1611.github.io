About Me

My name is Le Huy. I'm an undergraduate student in Computer Science at HCMIU-VNU, focusing on Artificial Intelligence. I'm on my way to explore the world in every aspect through my viewpoint lens by becoming a researcher. My primary research areas are video understanding and vision+language.

I'm a lazy guy who tries to find the reason and meaning before doing anything with the believe that meaningless actions lead to meaningless persons. I believe that everyone should “enjoy the roads not the goals" so when you’re doing something that makes you feel hard-working instead of effortlessly enjoying it, you may not done it the right way. I think my perspective have some what similar terminology called ”wu-wei or “the Flow" from “The Art of Not Trying - Lao Tzu"
